About Manisha

With over 15 years of dedicated service in Fredericton, Manisha Varma has been a strong voice for community, inclusion, and progress.

She serves as a board member of the Multicultural Association of Fredericton and the Association of Indo-Canadians, contributing actively to cultural growth and community development.
