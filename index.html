A Voice for the Community

For over 15 years, Manisha Varma has served Fredericton as an educator, cultural advocate, and community leader.

As a board member of (MCAF) Multicultural Association of Fredericton, NB and (AIC) Association Of Indo-Canadians, Fredericton NB, she has championed inclusivity, strengthened multicultural engagement, and empowered families across the city.

Her leadership is rooted in real impact — bringing people together and creating opportunities for all.
