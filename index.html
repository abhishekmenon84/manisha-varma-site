About the candidate

A voice for Ward 7

After thoughtful consideration and encouragement from her family, community members, and the organizations she has had the privilege to serve, Manisha Varma is proud to announce her candidacy for Ward 7 – Southwood Park Lincoln in Fredericton.

For over 15 years, Manisha has been deeply involved in the community as an educator, cultural advocate, and through the privilege of serving organizations such as the Multicultural Association of Fredericton, the Indo-Canadian Association, and the Asian Heritage Society in leadership roles. Her journey has always been rooted in service — supporting families, empowering youth, and creating spaces where everyone feels included.

Manisha is committed to representing Ward 7 with dedication, collaboration, and a strong voice. She will work to enhance public services, advocate for better healthcare and education, champion housing for the vulnerable, and build inclusive community spaces informed by her background in performing arts and community programming.

Key issues for Ward 7

Six clear commitments to make Ward 7 – Southwood Park Lincoln a better place for every family, newcomer, and long-time resident.

🏛️

Recreation & inclusive spaces

Drawing on her experience in performing arts and community programming, Manisha is committed to enhancing recreational facilities and building inclusive community spaces where every resident belongs.

  • Upgraded recreational facilities
  • Performing arts & cultural programming
  • Accessible spaces for all ages & backgrounds
Priority
🏠

Housing the vulnerable

Guided by her direct experience supporting housing initiatives through volunteer organizations, Manisha will advocate for solutions that shelter those most at risk and keep families in our community.

  • Affordable housing development
  • Support for vulnerable residents
  • Collaboration with housing organizations
🏥

Healthcare & education

Informed by her background as an educator and her involvement in improving healthcare access in Fredericton, Manisha will collaborate with partners and other levels of government to deliver better outcomes.

  • Improved healthcare access
  • Education advocacy & support
  • Cross-government collaboration
⚙️

Efficient public services

Drawing on over 15 years of leadership and volunteer experience, Manisha will ensure public services in Ward 7 are more efficient, responsive, and accessible to every resident who needs them.

  • Streamlined city services
  • Better resident responsiveness
  • Transparent local governance
👨‍👩‍👧

Families & youth

Manisha's journey has always been rooted in supporting families and empowering youth. She will ensure Ward 7 has the programs and services that give every young person a strong start in life.

  • After-school & youth programs
  • Safe parks and recreation spaces
  • Support for working families
🌍

Community & inclusivity

As a cultural advocate who has championed diversity for decades, Manisha will ensure Ward 7 is a place where every resident — regardless of background — feels welcomed and represented at City Hall.

  • Multicultural community programming
  • Cultural celebration & advocacy
  • Strong voice for newcomers
